Welcome to the AWS Cloud Guesser Game!

Are you ready to test your knowledge of Amazon Web Services? In this game, you will have to guess the correct AWS services selected by the game. Let's get started!

How to Play:

1. The game randomly selects 3 AWS services from a list of 10 generated services. Your goal is to identify and place the 3 correct services in the designated spots.

2. At the top of screen, there are 3 empty rectangles, which are placeholders for the selected services.

3. At the bottom of the screen, you will see 10 rectangles, each containing an AWS service.

4. Your task is to choose which services you think are the correct ones then drag and drop the AWS services from the bottom rectangles into the 3 empty rectangles at the top.

5. After placing your guesses, press the "Check" button to verify if your guesses are correct.

6. If your guess is correct, the game will display the number of attempts and the time taken to complete the game.

7. You can view the leader board by clicking the leader board icon.

8. Click the "Restart" button to start a new game.

Tip: Pay attention to the details and try to make your guesses quickly!
